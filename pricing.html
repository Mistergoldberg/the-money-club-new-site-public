Where Tuition Goes

The Money Club.Org is a nonprofit summer program designed around learning, not upselling.

We share a simple budget breakdown so families can see how tuition supports instruction, materials, venue costs, insurance, and program operations. The goal is not to maximize profit. The goal is to run a thoughtful, well-supported program for students in a transparent and responsible way.

A practical note

The Money Club.Org is a nonprofit startup. We are building this program to support community spirit, practical knowledge-sharing, and local economic participation through tested frameworks and systems thinking.

To run the program responsibly, we share our planning budget openly so families can see where tuition goes.

We share this openly because transparency is part of the model. The goal is not to maximize profit, but to build a fair, useful, community-rooted program.

Help us reach our goal — and see whether your son or daughter is interested in building something real this summer at UTSU Student Commons.

Refund Policy
Because The Money Club.Org is a lean nonprofit startup, program fees are non-refundable. Each registration directly supports the costs of running the program, including venue, materials, and University of Toronto student mentor wages.
